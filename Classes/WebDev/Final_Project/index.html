How to play

GOAL: capture all of your opponent's checkers or position your pieces so that your opponent has no available moves.
Pieces are always moved diagonally in the forward direction (towards the opponent) to the next dark square.
If there is one of the opponent's pieces next to a piece and an empty space on the other side, you jump your opponent and remove their piece. **REFRESH to Restart
