Ways of Seeing Reflection

Technology has expanded how the art world is view. Art is available to be viewed on the internet and as replicas in other museums and stores. Technology has allowed people from all over the world to view the art piece without having to travel to where the piece is being shown. Like with the Da Vinci sketch, technology allows the preservation of art. When the sketch is placed in light for it to be viewed, the light causes the sketch to fade losing the detail it once had. Using cameras to get detailed images of the piece before it loses all of its details and beauty.
